    Welcome to TTRose!

    Toontown Rose is a project that will revive the original TTO Client using nothing but OTP, The Original TTO Client and Launcher!

    Is it ready?

    Toontown Rose is not ready and is still being prepared, You can keep track of the progress at our official discord server!

    Our Goal

    Our goal is to make the TTO Launcher start TTO And allow it to connect to the server, make a toon and play around with friends! Our Goal has started ever since TTO First closed and now it is time for us to make the dream come true.
